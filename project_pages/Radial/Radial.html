Pitch, Context & Trivia

Radial is a 2D speedrun oriented platformer in which you will be able to use teleportation portals. Be ready for acrobatic moves, fights against creatures, hidden challenges and race against the clock! Will you master the portals powers and take up the challenge?.
Radial is my team first "long" project, we spent 9 weeks during the production phase of our first bachelor year, then we decided to spend our holidays time to improve the game, double the amount of levels and ship our game on Steam, for free of course! This was wonderful experience, we learnt a lot, especially about team work, production, milestone, deadline managment, build frequence (never build before the milestone haha), shipping procedure, and even more!
Radial was prensent on the 2019 Paris Games Week too!
