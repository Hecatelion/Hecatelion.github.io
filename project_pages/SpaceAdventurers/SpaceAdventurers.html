Pitch, Context & Trivia

A little party game in which Tom and Luna, two kids playing in their room try to make their space ship reach the moon, fighting aliens and repairing machines!
This was the second Jam I participated but the first Global Game Jam! For this occasion my team and I had decided to do it working with Unreal Engine to improve our understanding of this engine, that at this moment we didn't know well. We had chosen to create a casual party game so that it will be easy to understand and playable by most of the players. The theme announced was "Repair" and we wanted to make a game where kids were just having fun, such as us, grown kids having fun in a jam!

What did I do?

As a Programmer

  • interaction with some props, throwing and catching elements
  • timer, UI and win/lose condition
  • learning blueprint and UE4
  • surviving blueprint (this was kinda new and time were constantly running out)

As a Designer

  • brainstorming leading
  • game concept and theme proposals
  • working on affordance (hastily)
  • bedroom modules' repartition (level design) (hastily)
  • partnership with another group : one challenge of this GGJ was "having common universe with another jam group", I noticed that another group was making a card game so we put some easter eggs in their games and they do the same on their side!
