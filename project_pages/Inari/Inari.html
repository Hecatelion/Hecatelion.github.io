Pitch, Context & Trivia

A dark technology powered by a mysterious energy is corumpting human-beings soul, as Inari, last kami alive, your mission is to climb the power were the mysterious energy source is kept. Pass multiple enigmatic rooms, use your duplication powers to establish a delayed solo cooperation gameplay, and discover the majesty of this evil tower.
Inari was our second long time and unity project, we tried to develop a game more accessible than Radial, with a slow pace, an easier difficulty level and way more soft difficulty curve.
Inari was prensent on the 2019 Paris Games Week too!
