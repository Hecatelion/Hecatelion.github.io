Painting Memories

Game Design Document (eng)

Characteristics

  • type : Point&Click, Multiple-choice Story Game
  • team : 1 game & level designer
  • dev duration : 41 days

Pitch, Context & Trivia

In Painting Memories, you'll be focused on a child evolution, knowing his everyday-life advendures through his dreams. You'll be able to select in his recent memories to create, paint his long time memories.
I worked on this GDD within the scope of an exam, the instructions was to do a 17 pages document covering a GDD + a dissertation + a portefolio. The theme was "La Jetée" a movie produced by Chris Marker in 1962.

What did I do?

As a Game & Level Designer

  • analysing "La Jetée" to get the experience provided by the film
  • thinking about several game concepts and chosing the most relevant one
  • writing all the Game Design document in english then translating it in french
  • writing scenario
  • listing different story branches
  • writing characters personnalities and role
  • artistic direction proposal
  • morals of the story linked to gameplay items
  • technological constraints
  • marketing target and economical constraints
